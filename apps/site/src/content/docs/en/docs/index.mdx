**schakl.** is a self-hosted platform for agencies: clients, contacts, projects, hours,
leave and tasks in one fast web app that also installs as an app on your phone (PWA). You
run it on your own server, under your own brand and domain.

## The client is the centre

Everything in schakl. attaches to the **client**: contacts, projects, domains, websites,
hosting, hours and agreements. The client page shows a panel per topic — disable a module
and its panel disappears. One page keeps giving the whole picture, however large your
installation grows.

## Modular from the core

Every capability is a **module** you enable or disable per organization (*Settings →
Modules*). Each module brings its own screens, permissions and settings. What you don't
use, you never see.

## Your language, your brand

The whole app defaults to Dutch, with English built in; an extra language is one
translation file. Logo, colors, brand name and custom domain live under *Settings →
Branding* — no rebuild required.
